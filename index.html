Our Features

We create a direct link between farmers and manufacturers, cutting costs and promoting sustainability. Our eco-friendly packaging solutions are made from agricultural waste, turning waste into valuable resources. We also provide an additional revenue stream for farmers.

How It Is Made

The process starts from transforming agriculture residues into paper pulp for the packaging industry. ‘Eco-Pulping’ is about saving resources and using waste otherwise left to rot or burn and cause emissions. The next step, like traditional, existing methods, is a mechanical process to refine, dewater, clean, and screen material.

Why Enviro Case

Enviro Case is dedicated to providing sustainable packaging solutions by transforming agricultural waste into high-quality, eco-friendly packaging. We are committed to innovation, sustainability, and delivering value to both farmers and manufacturers.
